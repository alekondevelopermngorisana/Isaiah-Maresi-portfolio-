About Me

Knowing Isn't Enough.
I Help You Do.

For most people, there's a wide gap between what they know and what they actually do. I help young people, graduates, and emerging leaders close that gap — moving from potential to performance using my ACTION Model: Awareness → Clarity → Translation → Implementation → Ownership → Navigation.

As Sustainable Livelihoods Officer at SHOFCO, I train and equip thousands of youth with employability and entrepreneurship skills, while mentoring emerging leaders and supporting community-based savings groups across Nairobi's informal settlements.
